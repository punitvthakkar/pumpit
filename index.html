Pump It!

How to Play:

You'll be presented with a series of balloons, one at a time.

For each balloon, you have two choices:

  • Pump: Increases the balloon size and adds points (5 points per pump).
  • Cash Out: Banks your current points and moves to the next balloon.

Be careful! If you pump too much, the balloon will pop and you'll lose points for that balloon.

Important: Different colored balloons have different capacities. Pay attention to the colors and adapt your strategy accordingly!
